Privacy Policy

We care about your privacy, because we care about ours too.

Lawyer-ish texts are confusing, so our Privacy Policy uses simple language.

We do not sale any personally identifiable data. Ever.

This Privacy Policy applies to all our services.

Information Gathering and Usage

When using our built-in support system we ask for information such as your name and email address. The sole purpose is to provide help and support to anyone who has installed and using the extension.

We also store the saved settings for each visited website of each Night Eye user in their localStorage. This relates to all adjustments made by the user to improve their browsing experience through the use of Night Eye. There are 7 types of adjustments: Colors, Images, Brightness, Contrast, Saturation, Cold/Warm and Dim.

We do NOT collect anonymous usage data using an industry standard third party analytics (Google Analytics) nor other tools built by us while using the extension. We collect anonymous usage data using an industry standard third party analytics service (Google Analytics) only on the website nighteye.app. The information we collect via third party services does not contain personal information and its sole purpose is to improve the usability of the website.

Your Data

We may use third party partners in order to be able to provide you with our services. We own all rights to the information related to our services (e.g. codes, images, videos, texts, databases), but you retain all rights to your personal data.

Changes

Changes to our Privacy Policy will be made available on this page, and will be made at our own discretion. We can provide the history of changes to you if you request so. Your continued use of our services constitute your acceptance of such changes.
